5

How to make Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • A pinch of salt
  • Vanilla essence*

Recipe

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract if using.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined (the batter may be slightly lumpy).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with syrup or toppings of your choice!
